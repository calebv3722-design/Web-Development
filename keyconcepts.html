Key Concept

Cookies

Cookies are small fragments of data that are stored in your browser by websites that could remember stuff about you. This could be your status of being logged in, the product(s) in your shopping cart or that you like dark mode. Cookies are what make sites in the internet feel easier and personal, yet they may gather more information than individuals may even think about it. Due to that, they have become a significant aspect of the debate on online privacy.
